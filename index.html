Our Story

AlHamdan & Alaamri is a Law Firm based in Saudi Arabia which offers a full range of legal services for both national and international clients. We offer first-tier legal services that are both efficient and integrated via a team of top Saudi Arabian and international qualified attorneys that provide assistance in a variety of practice areas.

Arbitration is an alternative mean of dispute resolution that differs from the process of resolving a dispute through a lengthy and costly trial. Our team has dedicated its efforts in preparing and providing numerous consultations, studies, contracts, and regulation in relation to employees and employment in accordance with the Saudi Labour Law.
